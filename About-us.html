ABOUT US

Child Freedom Fund Works in over 150 countries all around the world,
to protect the right and freedom of every child.

Child Freedom Fund is a non-governmental organization that was founded with the goal of promoting the welfare and right of children around the world. The organization was started by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the need for concerted effort to address the numerous challenges faced by children, particularly those from marginalized communities.

As the organization grew and expanded its activities, the use of collective pronoun "we" became more prevalent in its communication and outreach.
This shift now reflects the fact that the Child Freedom Fund is now a team of dedicated professionals and volunteers who works tirelessly to support children in need.

Overall, the Child Freedom Fund's success is a testament to the power of collective action and the commitment of individual, to make positive difference in the lives of children.

Child Freedom Fund's Mission

The mission of the Child's Freedom Fund is to promote the welfareand and the rights of children around the world by providing them with education, healthcare, social services and advocating for child protection policies
