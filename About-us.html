ABOUT US

Child Freedom Fund Works in over 150 countries all around the world, to protect the right and freedom of every child.

Children's Freedom Fund is a non-governmental organization that was founded with the goal of promoting the welfare and right of children around the world. The organization was started by a group of passionate individuals who recognized the need for concerted effort to address the numerous challenges faced by children, particularly those from marginalized communities.
As the organization grew and expanded its activities the use of collective pronoun “we” became more prevalent in its communication and outreach. This shift now reflects the fact that the Child Freedom Fund is now a team of dedicated professional and volunteers who works tirelessly to support children in need. Read more

Our Mission

The mission of the Child's Freedom Fund is to promote the welfare and the rights of children around the world by providing them with education, healthcare, social services, and advocating for child protection polices

Our Values

We are guided by the following values:

-Child-centeredness: The organization place the need and right of children at the forefront of all activities.

-Equality and diversity: The organization promotes equality and respect for diversity.

-Love and care We show: love to all while embracing care wholeheartedly and compassionately.
