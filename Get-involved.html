BECOME A CORPORATE PARTNER

The support and help we receive from our corporate partners is hugely important to us and we are always seeking to form new partnerships to help us to impact more childrens' lives. Find out more about a partnership with Street Child . We are dedicated to making a difference in the world and we believe that working together we can achieve our mission. learn more

LEAVE A GIFT IN YOUR WILL

By leaving Street Child a gift in your Will, you will be helping to transform the lives of children and their families in low resource environments and emergencies. We recognize that every child deserves to be loved and cared for, a small gift can bring joy and comfort to a child that might be struggling learn more

GRANT GIVERS & FUNDING PARTNERS

We work closely with a network of 95+ local organisations to bring about low-cost and long-lasting change for the most vulnerable, especially those living in precarious, poor, and/or peripheral populations in low resource and emergency contexts. We welcome all contributions and support, whether its through donating funds, providing grants for our programs. learn more
